About

My number one passion is programming. What drives me the most, is my urge to create something that has the potential to be useful and life changing for others. In short: create something that I can be proud of.
When I first discovered programming back in grade seven, I could immediately imagine myself becoming a professional in this industry.

However, apart from professional skills I also possess significant interpersonal and social skills. Having played Tennis nationally in my home country, I proved my self-discipline, my eagerness to work hard for success and my ability to integrate into a team.
If you are looking for a hard working individual, who is not only able to work well in a team environment but is also good at coding, contact me! You can find my contact details below.
